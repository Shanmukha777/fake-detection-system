What is fake detection


The fake detection system performs binary classification of various of pictures available with the help of concepts pertaining to Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. 

Our goal

 
The goal of this system is to identify the fake images and videos . It is AI TOOL to stop women crimes and child abuse.
We aim to provide the user with the ability to classify the images as fake or real .
